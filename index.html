NOTES FOR TESTING

Thanks for testing!

Shoot me an email with any feedback @ MK.BARRIAULT@outlook.com

Specific Feedback I'm looking for:

  • What device did you use? Mobile, PC/Desktop, Mac, etc (if mobile, which phone?)
  • If you used a Mac, did it work? (I've only tested on a windows machine)
  • Are controls intuitive?
  • Did you beat it?
  • Is it too hard, too easy, or a decent balance of challenging but beat-able?
  • Did you notice any lagging? If so, where? (Some visual elements are fairly heavy on the GPU)

Optional: Collect data that will be super helpful for balancing stats!

  • After the opening scene - press the backtick key (`)
  • A little panel will open on the right (pictured below)
  • Hit "Start" under "Session Recorder" and press the backtick again
  • Continue playing
  • Once you're done, hit the backtick one more time
  • Click "Stop + Export" - This will download a .json file
  • Email the file to MK.BARRIAULT@outlook.com

Controls

🖥️ DESKTOP

  • WASD / ←↑↓→ – Move
  • SPACE – Lasers
  • Q / E – Barrel roll
  • Right Shift – Speed boost
  • F – Singularity Bomb

📱 MOBILE

  • 🕹️ Joystick – Move
  • A – Lasers
  • B – Barrel roll
  • X – Speed boost
  • Y – Singularity Bomb

This version is the development version so there are a couple extra features that you can check out:

  • In the Session Recorder Panel, there's also a "Playtest Bot" - if you click "Toggle Bot", a bot will start playing. He's actually pretty good and kinda fun to watch
  • The "TO THE WORM" button takes you straight to the final boss battle
  • "Tentacle Lab" is a fun little sandbox - A few of the enemies have proceedurally created tentacles. I used the lab to figure out the physics and aesthetic for each of the tentacle-based creatures. It's fun to move the sliders to extreme values and watch the tentacles go crazy. I ended up using this concept for another game that's in super early development, Tentacles. Feel free to check it out! PW is "HunterBiden"
